How to Generate PNG for LinkedIn

  1. Click one of the "Download PNG" buttons above (300x300 recommended)
  2. The PNG will be downloaded to your Downloads folder
  3. Go to LinkedIn Developers
  4. Select your app
  5. Click Settings tab
  6. Scroll to App Logo section
  7. Click Upload Logo
  8. Select the downloaded PNG file
  9. Click Save

Alternative Method: Screenshot

  1. Right-click on the logo above
  2. Select "Save Image As..." or "Copy Image"
  3. Save as PNG format
  4. Upload to LinkedIn Developer Portal

Requirements

  • ✅ Format: PNG or JPG
  • ✅ Minimum Size: 100x100 pixels
  • ✅ Recommended: 300x300 pixels
  • ✅ Aspect Ratio: Square (1:1)
  • ✅ File Size: Under 5MB
